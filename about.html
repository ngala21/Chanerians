Our History

Founded in 1974, Chania High School has been a cornerstone of education in the region for over 50 years. What began as a small institution with just 150 students has grown into a thriving educational community of over 1,200 students.

Throughout the decades, we have maintained our commitment to academic excellence while adapting to the changing needs of our students and society. Our facilities have expanded to include state-of-the-art laboratories, modern classrooms, comprehensive sports facilities, and a well-stocked library.

Today, Chania High School stands as a testament to the power of quality education, producing graduates who have gone on to excel in various fields including medicine, engineering, law, arts, and public service.
